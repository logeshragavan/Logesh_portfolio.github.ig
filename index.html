Project

Role: Prototype Design

Title: Automatic Fruit Quality Inpection System

Description: In this digital world, color processing gives us a greater impact in different industries to solve the consistency issue of continuous manual sorting. Nowadays, image or colors processing attract massive attention as it leads to possibility of widening scope of application in different field with the help of modern technology. Color sorting the fruit is researched, designed and created with Arduino Uno microcontroller, TCS300 color sensor, servo motor and other electronic components. This work involves sensors that sense the fruit color and sends the signal to the Arduino. The microcontroller sends signal to circuit which drives the servo motors to sort the fruit from the conveyor. Based upon the detection, the Fruit allowed to the specified location, or sorted the fruit and servo comes back to the original position. The system has the ability to sort the Fruit according to their colors into respective quality station in minimum time.
